About Caribe Resort

Vacation in luxury in this gorgeous 3/3 condo at Caribe Resort!! This unit features an oversized living area and a huge deck with plenty of seating for relaxing as you look out towards the Gulf of Mexico. The deck also overlooks the lazy river area at Caribe! Upon entering the Resort through the 24 hour guarded gate, you'll enter a 23 acre paradise of lush natural landscaping and Caribbean styling that sets a special mood for your most relaxing and fun filled vacation ever. Caribe's amenities are world class. The kids will love the water slides at the three outdoor pools and everyone will enjoy floating on the 'Lazy River.' Indoor pools, jacuzzies, saunas, steam rooms, fitness center, tennis courts and a 10,000 square foot golf putting green are just a short elevator ride away. Feel the fresh salt air, and enjoy the awesome sunrises and sunsets. You will love being able to access free wireless high speed internet. Caribe is the ultimate vacation! Master Bedroom has 1 king bed, First Guest Bedroom has 1 queen bed, Second Guest Bedroom has a queen bed and twin bunk bed. Unit also has a queen sleeper sofa. **Additional Rental Information** Policy prohibits rental to adults under the age of 25 Check in after 4pm and Check out at 10am NO SMOKING IN UNIT OR ON THE BALCONY ONLY IN DESIGNATED AREAS ON THE COMPLEX. NO Pets
